  • a) Identity Data: includes username, password, and other identifiers you may use on our Services.

  • d) Usage Data: includes information about how you use our Services, such as the types of content you view or engage with, the features you use, the actions you take, and the time, frequency, and duration of your activities.

2.2 Non-Personal Data

We also collect and use Aggregated Data such as statistical or demographic data for any purpose. Aggregated Data could be derived from your Personal Data but is not considered Personal Data in law as this data will not directly or indirectly reveal your identity.

3. How We Collect Your Information

We use different methods to collect data from and about you, including:

  • a) Direct interactions: You may give us your Identity and Contact Data by filling in forms or by corresponding with us by email or otherwise.
